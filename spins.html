See below the different routes the club takes each week from leisure to advanced spins. You are welcome to attend 1-2 group cycles to try it out. Please note that after your trial period you will need to register for membership with Cycling Ireland & The Twelve Bens CC. The correct safety equipment is required for all cycles: front and rear light, helmet. Suitable rain gear is advised no matter how nice it may look, it is ireland after all and we can have all 4 season in one day



Leisure Spin 30km

Leisure cycles are a steady introduction. You can use any type of bike for this cycle as it is not about speed just getting you used to the bike. The instructor will always try to bring you on a level spin so you donâ€™t have to climb as much. This can be hard in Connemara so it may not be a loop just out and back the same way.



Beginner Spin 30km +

The beginner route is the next step up it is advisable to have a road bike for this spin as it will be a little faster than the leisure. Along the route the instructor will introduce you to some tips and tricks for pedalling, hand & body position and formation cycling. These can seem complicated at first but will become second nature eventually.

Advanced Spin 60km +

This spin is not for the faint hearted. It is fast paced and you will need a high level of fitness to be able to keep up. The tips and trick you picked up in the beginner group will come into practice here. These spins can go up to 100km at an average speed of 30km/h.
