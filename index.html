About

The twelve bens cycling club operates out of Clifden county Galway. The club is in fact named after the Connemara mountain range and not because we have 12 members named Ben. The club has over 30 members from the area and we aim to meet up twice a week for training and attend different cycling events nationwide.

The twelve bens cycling club is for anyone who enjoys cycling or would like to get into it. There really is a spin for all levels. The club aims to have group cycles at least twice a week in the summer and once a week in the winter months. Once you get to know some people you may be able to set up cycles with other members at other times during the week.

The picturesque landscapes are a magnificent backdrop to your ride. The area is filled with routes for all levels of cyclist. Most of the routes are full loops so you can end up right where you started. You can even cycle the same routes as the tour de Conamara you can click the link here to find out more and perhaps enter.

The club also hosts and takes part in a number of cycling challenges throughout the most infamous is the sky road challenge 12laps of the sky road loop 204km 2500 meters of climbing are you up for the challenge? We love cycling and if we can help a good cause by cycling we are in. Get in touch if you have an idea or would like to donate to one of our chosen charities.

The club is always full of activity please head over to our social media pages via the links below to check out the latest news. Also if you are interested in joining the club or just dropping by complete the sign up page to be added to the WhatsApp group.
